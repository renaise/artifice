## About

### About Artifice

Artifice is an artist-centered cultural organization working across public programming, publishing, and community infrastructure.

We support artists and audiences through events, editorial initiatives, and shared resources that create access, dialogue, and long-term cultural value.

### Mission

Artifice develops platforms for cultural exchange through programming, publishing, and community-centered initiatives.

Artists and cultural workers need shared infrastructure: spaces, conversations, and resources that support experimentation, connection, and sustained engagement.
